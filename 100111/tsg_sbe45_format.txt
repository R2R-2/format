Data Format Description: These files contain continuous sea surface measurements including temperature, conductivity, salinity, and sound velocity. The data is recorded as comma-separated values preceded by a log identifier and a high-resolution ISO 8601 UTC timestamp.

Standards Followed: ISO 8601 (Timestamping), ITS-90 (Temperature), PSS-78 (Practical Salinity Scale 1978), UNESCO (Sound Velocity)

Useful References:

Sample Rate: 0.2 Hz (one sample every 5 seconds)

Data line column descriptions (one description per unique line format):

Example Line: tsg_sbe45_fwd    2025-09-07T17:01:06.1660Z     21.5316,  0.00503,   0.0307, 1487.000
where:
  tsg_sbe45_fwd       Log Name: Identifier for the forward thermosalinograph unit.
  2025-09-07T...      Log Time: ISO 8601 UTC timestamp (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS.ssssZ).
  21.5316             Temperature: Water temperature inside the TSG (Â°C).
  0.00503             Conductivity: Electrical conductivity of the seawater (S/m).
  0.0307              Salinity: Practical Salinity (psu).
  1487.000            Sound Velocity: Speed of sound through water (m/sec).
