tsg_sbe45.txt

  Sea-Bird SBE 45 MicroTSG Conductivity and Temperature Monitor.
  Measures surface seawater temperature and conductivity.

  Model: SBE45
  Sample rate: 1 every 5 seconds

  Example data line:
    tsg_sbe45_fwd  2014-12-12T06:34:03.1647Z   26.4679,  5.44818,  34.9076, 1537.771

  Field Descriptions:
    Logger ID
    Time Stamp [UTC]
    Temperature [C]
    Conductivity [S/m]
    Salinity [psu]
    Speed of Sound [m/s]
